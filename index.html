Our Story

Two Decades, One Flame: The Journey of Chef Babar

They say a great kabab isn’t just made with spice; it’s made with time. For Chef Babar, that journey began over 20 years ago. After two decades behind the tandoors of some of the most respected kitchens, Babar decided it was time to bring his own vision of "Ujala" (light) to the plate. With every skewer he prepares, Babar draws on a lifetime of experience—perfecting the precise moment the meat becomes tender, the exact temperature of the clay oven, and the secret spice blends that have become his signature. Today, Chef Babar has a simple, humble mission: to serve the world, one happy stomach at a time. Whether you are a regular or a first-time visitor, you aren’t just a customer in his kitchen—you are a guest at his table.
